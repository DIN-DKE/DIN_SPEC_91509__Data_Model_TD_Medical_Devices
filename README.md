## Overview of DIN SPEC 91509

DIN SPEC 91509 defines a structured, interoperable data model for representing the Technical Documentation of medical devices. Its objective is to translate regulatory requirements‚Äîparticularly in the context of the EU MDR‚Äîinto a consistent, machine-readable structure.

### Core Objectives

- Standardization of the structure of technical documentation  
- Improved traceability and consistency of regulatory content  
- Enablement of digital workflows and automation  
- Promotion of interoperability between manufacturers, service providers, and authorities  

The specification addresses the growing complexity of regulatory requirements by establishing a shared semantic foundation for digital documentation processes.

## Role of MDKU e.V.

**MDKU e.V. (Medica Device Knowledge Units e.V.)** initiated and significantly coordinated the development of DIN SPEC 91509.

The association brings together manufacturers, software providers, consultancies, and other stakeholders to develop and implement digital standards within the medical device sector.

DIN SPEC 91509 was developed through an open, consensus-based process involving experts from industry and regulatory domains. The objective was to create a technically robust and practically applicable specification.
